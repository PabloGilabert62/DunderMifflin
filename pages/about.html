Dunder Mifflin Paper Company, Inc.
Who We Are
Dunder Mifflin Paper Company, Inc. is a trusted American supplier of premium paper solutions for businesses, offices, and institutional clients. Since 1949, we have focused on delivering reliable, high-quality paper products supported by customer-first service and a strong commitment to long-term business relationships.
Our History
Founded in Scranton, Pennsylvania, Dunder Mifflin began as a small regional vendor supplying paper to local companies and government organizations. Over the decades, we expanded our reach, evolving into a nationwide provider known for consistency, service quality, and genuine human-centered support.
Despite technological shifts and major industry changes, our company has remained resilient thanks to three core principles:
A commitment to unmatched product quality. Personalized customer care. A workplace culture that values teamwork, integrity, and dedication.
Our Mission
To deliver dependable, sustainable, and accessible paper solutions while prioritizing human connection in every client interaction.
Our Vision
To become the most trusted paper supplier in the United States, recognized for service excellence, operational integrity, and a strong organizational culture.
Our Values
Quality above all. Integrity in every transaction. Customer-focused service. Practical, responsible innovation. Respect, transparency, and collaboration.
What We Do
Legal firms requiring secure, high-volume document management. Large manufacturing plants needing durable industrial packaging materials. Government and educational institutions with strict reliability and compliance standards. Retail and distribution centers seeking consistent supply for day-to-day operations. Corporate offices in need of premium, uniform-quality printing and filing products. Our approach prioritizes efficiency, supply-chain reliability, and personalized account support for every client—regardless of size.
Why Choose Dunder Mifflin?
Nationwide distribution capabilities. Quick delivery and flexible logistics. Competitive pricing for all business tiers. Dedicated account managers. Certified product quality and sustainability standards. Strong after-sales support.
